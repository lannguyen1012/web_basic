Gimbap

Gimbap, also romanized as kimbap, is a Korean dish made from cooked rice, vegetables, fish, and meat rolled in gim—dried sheets of seaweed—and served in bite-sized slices.

Most Popular Food

Frech-fries

French fries are a popular dish around the world. They are made from fresh potatoes, cut into long strips, and then deep-fried until they become crispy and golden.

Gỏi Thái

Gỏi Thái, or Thai Salad, is a traditional dish from Thai cuisine. It is made from fresh ingredients such as raw vegetables, chicken or shrimp, onions, garlic, chili peppers, fish sauce, and various spices. Thai Salad typically has a tangy, spicy, sweet taste and is often garnished with roasted peanuts, fried shallots, and herbs.

Đậu phụ chiên

Fried Tofu is made by deep-frying cubes or slices of tofu until they turn golden brown and crispy on the outside, while remaining soft and creamy on the inside. The tofu is usually marinated in a flavorful mixture of soy sauce, garlic, and other spices before being fried to enhance its taste.

Súp cua

The soup is typically prepared by simmering crab shells and various vegetables, such as onions, carrots, and celery, in a flavorful broth. As the ingredients meld together, the essence of the crab infuses the soup, creating a savory and comforting dish.

Our Customers Review

Gordon Ramsay

Gordon Ramsay is a culinary force to be reckoned with. His cooking skills are unparalleled, crafting beautifully presented dishes that are a delight to the senses. With his fiery temper and brutal honesty, Ramsay has made a name for himself as a no-nonsense chef. Beneath his tough exterior, there lies a genuine passion for food and a commitment to mentorship. His mentorship on shows like "Hell's Kitchen" and "MasterChef" has helped aspiring chefs reach their full potential. However, his aggressive demeanor can be overwhelming at times. Despite this, Ramsay's talent and dedication to his craft cannot be denied.

Guy Fieri

Guy Fieri, the flamboyant and energetic culinary personality. Known for his signature spiky hair and vibrant personality, Fieri has certainly made a name for himself in the food world. His love for bold flavors and hearty comfort food is evident in his cooking style. With his hit show "Diners, Drive-Ins and Dives," Fieri takes us on a cross-country journey, showcasing local dives and their mouthwatering dishes. He brings a sense of enthusiasm and genuine appreciation for the chefs and their creations. While some may find his style over-the-top, there's no denying his passion for food and his ability to entertain.

Giada De Laurentiis

Giada De Laurentiis, the enchanting Italian-American chef and television personality. With her warm smile, graceful demeanor, and undeniable talent in the kitchen, Giada has captured the hearts of many food enthusiasts around the world. Known for her expertise in Italian cuisine, she effortlessly combines traditional flavors with a modern twist, creating dishes that are both elegant and approachable. Her cooking shows, such as "Everyday Italian," have inspired countless home cooks to embrace the joys of Italian cooking. Giada's passion for food shines through as she shares her culinary knowledge with grace and authenticity, making her a beloved figure in the culinary world.
